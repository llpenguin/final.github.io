☃What is Crochet?

Crochet has been around for centuries and is still one of the most popular yarn crafts today.
It is a method of creating a looped fabric from yarn with a crochet.

☃What do you need to start crocheting?

One of the great things about crochet is that you don't need many tools
to get started. In fact, you only need these two things:
1. A crochet hook
2. Yarn
